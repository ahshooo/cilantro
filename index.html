Cilantro

Savior of salsas, defiler of soups.

Few foods are as divisive and polarizing as cilantro. The herb is revered as many times as it is cursed. This is a summary of its fame and infamy.

Taste

 

Lovers of cilantro describe its taste as:

 


Citrusy

Fragrant

 

Haters of cilantro compare its taste to:

 


Soap

Minty Cheese

Crushed Bugs

Dandelions


Bug Spray

Gym Socks

Metal

Rotten Eggs
